Design Principles

  • Clean white cards with subtle shadows — polished, not playful
  • Left accent bar for color coding — identifies node purpose at a glance
  • Sections separated by light dividers — scales from simple to detailed
  • Pill-shaped edge labels — lightweight, don't clutter the diagram
  • Light/dark theme support via CSS custom properties
